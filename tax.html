Direct Tax

Tax planning involves an intelligent application of the various provisions of the Direct Tax Laws to practical situations in such a manner as to reduce the tax impact to a minimum level. Our Expertise and thorough understanding of the principles, practices and procedures of tax laws and the ability to apply such knowledge to various practical situations helps just to do that.

Our return filing services include arrangement of your financial affairs in such a way that, without violating in any way the legal provisions, full advantage is taken of all tax exemptions, deductions, concessions, rebates, allowances and other reliefs or benefits permitted under the Act so that your burden of taxation is reduced to the minimum.

Indirect Tax

Indirect Tax is a type of tax where the incidence and impact of taxation does not fall on the same entity. Though most of the indirect taxes have been consolidated after implementation of GST however litigations with respect to the pre GST period are still in Vogue. At Borkar and Borkar our rich experience in Excise Service Tax and Vat coupled with our deep insight of the GST law provides the edge you require in your business.

Our client focus and broad exposure across various sectors of the economy helps to devise arrangements to create the least possible tax liability for your business.

We help you to:

  • Claim all the available exemptions
  • Availment of input tax credits
  • Claiming of Export Benefits
  • Evaluating your contracts
  • Evaluating your contracts for any possible consequences on GST
  • Filing of GST Returns
  • Hearing of Cases before GST/Excise/Service Tax and Vat Authorities
