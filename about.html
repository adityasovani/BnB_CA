About Borkar & Borkar

Borkar and Borkar CA was formed in 1983 by CA K. N Borkar and CA S.N Borkar with a view to provide top notch professional services in the fields of auditing and assurance, taxation and accouting. Since our inception we have emphasized on creating value for clients by focussing on solutions that are innovative yet practical, and that can be implemented.

This is achieved by combining domain expertise and maintaining an uncompromising focus on quality. By hiring and nurturing high quality professionals and articled assistants with a passion for excellence we deliver what is the best to our clients. Even after 36 years we at Borkar & Borkar Chartered Accountants strive to deliver highest quality of services and are committed to make a difference to clients and to its people.
