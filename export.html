Export Incentives

Foreign Trade Policy 2015-20 and other schemes have provided promotional measures to boost India’s exports with the objective to offset infrastructural inefficiencies and associated costs involved to provide exporters a level playing field. An exporter of goods and services is thus entitled to various export incentives- viz. Incentives under GST Refund Scheme, SEIS (Servicee Exports from India Scheme) MEIS (Merchandise Export from India Scheme) Duty Drawback, EPCG etc.

We at Borkar & Borkar provide you with end to end assistance right from claiming all the available export benefits to preparation of your legal documents and filing them with concerned revenue authorities to make sure that refunds are processed in the least possible time. We also provide advisory services on utilization of scrips and other incentives.

Our Services

  • GST Refund for Exporters
  • Assistance on Utilization of Duty Credit Scrips
  • Assistance on Sale of Duty Credit Scrips
  • Claiming Export Incentives
  • Preparation of Documents for Claiming export benefits
