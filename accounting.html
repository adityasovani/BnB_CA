Accounting Services

Accountancy is the practice of recording, classifying, and reporting on business transactions for a business. It provides feedback to management and stakeholders regarding the financial results and status of an organization.

Rapid pace of economic reforms in India has placed substantial demands on accounting teams of various business entities. Through Our never ending drive for excellence and fulfilling customer expectations we are able to add value to your business rather than just counting your money. Thus we have our reports ready when you need them. Our accounting staff is able to work across multiple Electronic platforms including Tally ERP, QuickBooks, and SAP ERP.

We provide some out of the box accounting services:

  • Accounting and Bookkeeping
  • Assistance in Compilation of Financial statements: Balance Sheet, Statement of Profit and Loss and Statement of Cash Flows
  • Consolidation of accounts
  • Assistance in Compliance of Indian GAAP
  • Recruitment advisory for Client Accounts and Finance Team
  • Accounting Advisory
  • Advisory on Implementation of accounting softwares and ERP Systems
